# Appeal to Novelty

It's new, therefore it's better. Innovation as inherent virtue. Disruption for disruption's sake.

**Examples:**
- "The old way is dying for a reason"
- "This changes everything"
- "We can't keep doing things the way we've always done them"
- "The future belongs to those who..."

**New is not improved. But the packaging is shinier.**
