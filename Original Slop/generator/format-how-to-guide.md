# How-To Guide

Instructional content for things that don't need instructions. Implies expertise. Delivers obvious advice dressed up as insight. The authority of a guide for the substance of a shrug.

## Headline Templates

- "How To [Simple Thing] Like A [Expert/Successful Person]"
- "The Ultimate Guide To [Thing Requiring No Guide]"
- "How To [Basic Skill] In [Year]"
- "[Number] Steps To [Obvious Goal]"
- "How To [Thing] (The Right Way)"
- "A Step-By-Step Guide To [Instinctive Action]"
- "How To Finally [Thing You Could Just Do]"
- "The Art Of [Mundane Activity]"

## Example Headlines

- "How To Send An Email Like A CEO"
- "The Ultimate Guide To Having A Meeting"
- "How To Breathe In 2026"
- "7 Steps To Forming An Opinion"
- "How To Leave Work On Time (The Right Way)"

## Article Structure

- Intro: Establish why this needs a guide (it doesn't)
- "Why this matters" section (inflated importance)
- Numbered steps that are either obvious or useless
- Pro tips that contradict the steps
- "Common mistakes" that are actually reasonable choices
- Conclusion with call to practice/implement
