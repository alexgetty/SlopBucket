# Profile

Character study of a type of person, presented as journalism about an individual. The subject is a archetype. The tone is documentary. The details are uncomfortably specific.

## Headline Templates

- "Meet The [Type Of Person] Disrupting [Industry]"
- "Inside The Mind Of The [Role/Type]"
- "A Day In The Life Of [Type Of Person]"
- "The [Type] Next Door"
- "What It's Really Like To Be A [Role]"
- "Portrait Of A [Type]: [Name], [Age]"
- "The Rise Of The [Type Of Person]"
- "This [Type] Makes $[Amount] Doing [Thing]"

## Example Headlines

- "Meet The Reply-All Enthusiast Disrupting Your Inbox"
- "Inside The Mind Of The LinkedIn Thought Leader"
- "A Day In The Life Of The Person Who Always Has A Meeting"
- "Portrait Of A Grinder: Blake, 27"
- "This Guy Makes $400K Giving Obvious Advice"

## Article Structure

- Lede placing subject in specific scene
- Quote that reveals too much
- Background/origin story
- "Typical day" section
- Industry/peer commentary
- Subject's philosophy (delusional)
- Closing scene that undercuts everything
