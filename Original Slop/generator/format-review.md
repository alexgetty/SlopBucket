# Review

Critical assessment of something that shouldn't be reviewed. Apply the review format to experiences, concepts, or abstract ideas. Stars/ratings optional but encouraged.

## Headline Templates

- "Review: [Abstract Concept] (★★☆☆☆)"
- "I Tried [Experience]. Here's My Honest Review."
- "[Thing] Review: [Number] Stars"
- "An Honest Review Of [Life Experience]"
- "[Concept]: A Review After [Time Period]"
- "Rating [Mundane Thing]: A Comprehensive Review"
- "I've Been Using [Life Situation] For [Years]. Here's What I Think."
- "[Thing] Review: The Good, The Bad, And The Pointless"

## Example Headlines

- "Review: Being An Adult (★★☆☆☆)"
- "I Tried Hustle Culture For A Year. Here's My Honest Review."
- "Mondays: A Review (1.5 Stars)"
- "An Honest Review Of My Own Personality"
- "Capitalism Review: Great Concept, Terrible Execution"

## Article Structure

- Intro: How you came to review this
- Pros section (struggle to find them)
- Cons section (extensive)
- Comparison to alternatives
- "Would I recommend it?" (reluctant or absurd answer)
- Final rating with arbitrary criteria
