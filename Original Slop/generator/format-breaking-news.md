# Breaking News

Urgent, breathless coverage of something that absolutely does not warrant it. The BREAKING tag implies immediacy and importance. The content delivers neither.

## Headline Templates

- "BREAKING: [Mundane Event Stated Dramatically]"
- "BREAKING: [Authority Figure] Confirms [Obvious Thing]"
- "DEVELOPING: [Situation] Continues To Be [Same Situation]"
- "JUST IN: [Non-News Framed As News]"
- "BREAKING: Sources Confirm [Vague Claim]"
- "URGENT: [Minor Inconvenience] Reaches Crisis Point"
- "BREAKING: [Thing] Officially [What It Already Was]"
- "LIVE UPDATES: [Static Situation] Remains Unchanged"

## Example Headlines

- "BREAKING: Local Man Has Opinion"
- "DEVELOPING: Meeting Could Have Been Email, Sources Confirm"
- "JUST IN: Area Woman 'Fine' Despite Saying Otherwise"
- "BREAKING: Study Finds Thing You Already Believed"
- "URGENT: Reply-All Chain Enters Third Hour"

## Article Structure

- Lead paragraph: State the "news" with maximum gravity
- Quote from unnamed source or expert
- Background context that adds nothing
- Developing updates that don't develop
- End with "This is a developing story" despite nothing developing
