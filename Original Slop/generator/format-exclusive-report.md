# Exclusive Report

Investigative journalism theater. The word "exclusive" implies access and revelation. The content delivers information available to anyone who bothered to look.

## Headline Templates

- "EXCLUSIVE: [Information That Isn't Exclusive]"
- "Inside [Thing]: An [Publication] Investigation"
- "EXCLUSIVE: [Source] Reveals [Open Secret]"
- "The Untold Story Of [Well-Documented Thing]"
- "[Publication] Investigation: [Obvious Finding]"
- "EXCLUSIVE: What [Entity] Doesn't Want You To Know (They Don't Care)"
- "Inside The Secret World Of [Mundane Thing]"
- "EXCLUSIVE: [Document] Obtained By [Publication]"

## Example Headlines

- "EXCLUSIVE: Inside The Slack Channels Where Decisions Get Unmade"
- "The Untold Story Of How Meetings Got So Long"
- "EXCLUSIVE: Sources Reveal CEO Also Finds Town Halls Boring"
- "Inside The Secret World Of People Who Leave On Time"
- "EXCLUSIVE: We Obtained The Company's Values Statement (It's On Their Website)"

## Article Structure

- Dramatic lede implying danger/secrecy
- "According to documents obtained by..."
- Quotes from anonymous sources (saying nothing)
- Unnecessary background that pads length
- Dramatic reveal that's anticlimactic
- Company/entity "did not respond to request for comment" (or did, boringly)
