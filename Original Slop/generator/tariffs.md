# Tariffs

Taxes on imports that the importing country's citizens actually pay, despite consistent framing that the exporting country is somehow footing the bill. A bipartisan favorite for sounding tough on trade while making your own citizens' groceries more expensive. The economic equivalent of punching yourself in the face to show the neighbor you're serious.
