# Prediction Markets

Platforms where people bet real money on future events, from elections to pandemics to whether their neighbor will finally fix that fence. Sold as "wisdom of crowds" but increasingly resembles "crowdsourced anxiety with a profit motive." The more people who can bet on catastrophe, the more people who need catastrophe.
