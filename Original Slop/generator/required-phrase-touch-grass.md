# Required Phrase: Touch grass

You must use this phrase at least once. Literal instruction to get offline and experience reality instead of doom-scrolling.

**Examples:**
- "The 47-tweet thread about why he's better than his competitors was at 2 AM. He needs to touch grass and sleep."
- "Her entire brand is arguing about fictional character love triangles in replies. She should touch grass."
- "They spent $8K on a seminar about optimizing their schedule and immediately forgot to go outside. Touch grass."
