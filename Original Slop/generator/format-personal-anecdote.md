# Format: Personal Anecdote Required

Must include a personal story that conveniently proves your point. The anecdote is the evidence.

**Structure:**
- Set the scene briefly
- The moment of realization
- The lesson that applies to everyone

**Examples:**
- "I was standing in the airport when it hit me..."
- "My daughter said something that changed everything..."
- "Last Tuesday, in a coffee shop in Austin..."
