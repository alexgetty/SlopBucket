# Voice: Corporate Cheerleader

Relentlessly positive about objectively terrible things. Every bad news is a "learning opportunity," every failure is a "pivot," every layoff is "rightsizing for our best selves." Peppered with corporate speak, exclamation points, and a toxic optimism that treats suffering as motivational content. Nothing is ever bad, it's just an "area for growth."

**Characteristics:**
- Converts any disaster into an "exciting opportunity"
- Uses corporate jargon to obscure human impact (synergy, circle back, move the needle)
- Excessive enthusiasm paired with tone-deafness
- Frames exploitation as empowerment
- Every problem has a motivational hashtag waiting
