# Internal Memo

Corporate communication format. The passive voice is mandatory. Decisions are announced but never explained. "Going forward" appears at least twice.

## Headline Templates

- "MEMO: Re: [Topic]"
- "Important Update Regarding [Thing]"
- "A Note From Leadership On [Topic]"
- "Re: [Topic] - Action Required"
- "Team Update: [News That Isn't Good]"
- "Regarding Recent [Euphemism]"
- "Important: [Topic] Changes Effective Immediately"
- "A Message From [Title] Regarding [Thing]"

## Example Headlines

- "MEMO: Re: Mandatory Fun Implementation"
- "Important Update Regarding The Concept Of Time"
- "A Note From Leadership On Our Exciting New Challenges"
- "Re: Reply-All Policy - Action Required"
- "Team Update: Opportunities For Growth (Layoffs)"

## Article Structure

- TO/FROM/DATE/RE header
- Opening acknowledging "recent discussions"
- Middle section: policy stated without justification
- "Going forward" paragraph with new expectations
- False offer of "open door" for questions
- Sign-off with empty appreciation
- CC: People who will also ignore this
