# Press Release

Corporate announcement format for things that don't warrant announcement. Maximum buzzwords, minimum news. The triumph of spin over substance.

## Headline Templates

- "[Company] Announces [Non-News]"
- "[Entity] Unveils [Thing That Already Existed]"
- "[Organization] Launches [Meaningless Initiative]"
- "[Company] Revolutionizes [Normal Activity]"
- "[Entity] Proudly Introduces [Minor Update]"
- "[Company] Reaffirms Commitment To [Vague Value]"
- "Introducing [Buzzword]: [Ordinary Thing Rebranded]"
- "[Company] Partners With [Other Company] To [Do Nothing New]"

## Example Headlines

- "TechCorp Announces Commitment To Being A Company"
- "Startup Unveils Revolutionary Way To Send Messages"
- "Company Launches Synergy 2.0"
- "Organization Proudly Introduces Breathing Room Initiative"
- "We Are Excited To Announce That We Have Announced Something"

## Article Structure

- Dateline and "FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE"
- Opening paragraph with all buzzwords front-loaded
- Quote from CEO saying nothing quotable
- Boilerplate about company's mission/vision
- Quote from partner saying same nothing
- "About [Company]" section
- Media contact (do not contact)
