# Emotional Beat: Despair to Hope

The article must start bleak and end optimistic. Classic redemption arc. Inspire through suffering.

**Structure:**
1. Open with a problem or dark moment
2. Middle: struggle and learning
3. End: breakthrough, hope, lessons learned

**Tone shift:** From "everything was falling apart" to "and that's when I realized"
