# Area Man/Woman

The Onion's signature format. A hyper-local story about a specific unnamed person doing something universally relatable or absurd. The specificity of "area" combined with the anonymity creates comedic distance.

## Headline Templates

- "Area Man [Mundane Action With Unearned Confidence]"
- "Area Woman [Relatable Struggle Stated As News]"
- "Area Man Convinced [Delusional Belief]"
- "Local Woman [Passive-Aggressive Action]"
- "Area Man [Action], Expects [Unrealistic Outcome]"
- "Local Man's [Ordinary Thing] Described As '[Grandiose Claim]'"
- "Area Woman [Does Thing] For [Absurd Reason]"
- "Area Man [Fails At Thing] With Complete Confidence"

## Example Headlines

- "Area Man Passionate Defender Of What He Imagines Constitution To Be"
- "Area Woman Could Have Sworn She Was More Interesting Than This"
- "Local Man Announces Plans To Change, Cites No Specific Timeline"
- "Area Man's Complete Lack Of Expertise No Barrier To Opinion"
- "Area Woman Pretty Sure Email Was Passive-Aggressive"

## Article Structure

- Lead: Introduce the area person and their situation
- Quote from the person (earnest, oblivious)
- Quote from friend/family member (contradicts or undermines)
- Backstory that makes it worse
- Kicker: The person's plan to continue exactly as before
