# Interview

Q&A format with someone who shouldn't be interviewed about the topic at hand. The subject's confidence inversely proportional to their expertise. The interviewer's questions increasingly desperate.

## Headline Templates

- "[Person/Role] On [Topic]: '[Pull Quote]'"
- "We Asked [Person] About [Topic]. The Answers Were [Adjective]."
- "[Title]: A Conversation About [Subject]"
- "'[Outrageous Quote]': An Interview With [Person]"
- "In Conversation: [Person] On [Everything]"
- "[Person] Opens Up About [Topic]"
- "The [Publication] Interview: [Person]"
- "[Person] Has Thoughts On [Topic]"

## Example Headlines

- "Intern On Corporate Strategy: 'I Have Ideas'"
- "We Asked A Reply-All Offender Why. The Answers Were Unhinged."
- "'Meetings Are My Love Language': An Interview"
- "LinkedIn Thought Leader Opens Up About Having No Thoughts"
- "Area Man Has Thoughts On The Economy"

## Article Structure

- Brief intro establishing subject's "credentials"
- Q&A format throughout
- Questions get more pointed as answers disappoint
- Subject contradicts themselves repeatedly
- Interviewer's frustration bleeds through
- End with subject promoting something irrelevant
