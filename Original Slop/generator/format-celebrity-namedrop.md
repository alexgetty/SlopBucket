# Format: Celebrity Name-Drop

Must reference a famous person to support your argument. Borrowed credibility.

**Examples:**
- "As Steve Jobs once said..."
- "Oprah understands this..."
- "I read that Bezos..."
- "There's a reason Beyonce..."

**Bonus points for misattributed quotes or taking them wildly out of context.**
