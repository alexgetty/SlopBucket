# The Birthday Reflection

An annual unloading of acquired wisdom triggered by one's arbitrary calendar day. The speaker frames the passing year as a personal transformation and imparts hard-won knowledge to followers, usually generic life lessons presented as if newly discovered.

**The formula:** "Today I'm [age]. Here's what I've learned..."

**Examples:**
- "I'm 30 now. I've learned that people will disappoint you and that's fine."
- "Another year older. I finally understand that working hard isn't enough."
- "Turning 35 taught me that nobody actually knows what they're doing."
