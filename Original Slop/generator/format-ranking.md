# Definitive Ranking

Arbitrary ranking of things that can't be objectively ranked. Presented with the confidence of scientific measurement. The criteria are made up. The order is provocative.

## Headline Templates

- "The Definitive Ranking Of [Category]"
- "Every [Thing] Ranked From Worst To Best"
- "[Number] [Things], Ranked"
- "We Ranked Every [Thing] And You Will Be Mad"
- "The Official [Category] Power Rankings"
- "Ranking All [Number] [Things]: A Definitive Guide"
- "The Only [Category] Ranking That Matters"
- "[Things] Ranked By Someone With No Qualifications"

## Example Headlines

- "The Definitive Ranking Of Excuses For Being Late"
- "Every Type Of Meeting, Ranked From Worst To Best"
- "All 7 Days Of The Week, Ranked"
- "We Ranked Every Email Sign-Off And You Will Be Mad"
- "The Official Coffee Order Power Rankings"

## Article Structure

- Intro establishing false authority to rank
- State criteria (vague or absurd)
- List from worst to best (or reverse)
- Brief justification for each ranking
- Acknowledge controversy while dismissing it
- End with #1 reveal (either obvious or unhinged)
