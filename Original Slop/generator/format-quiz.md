# Quiz/Assessment

The personality quiz format. Implies insight into self. Delivers confirmation of what you wanted to hear. The questions are leading. The results are flattering.

## Headline Templates

- "What Kind Of [Category] Are You?"
- "Take This Quiz To Find Out Your [Type]"
- "Which [Pop Culture Thing] Are You Based On [Behavior]?"
- "This Quiz Will Reveal Your [Hidden Trait]"
- "Answer These [Number] Questions To Discover Your [Thing]"
- "What Your [Preference] Says About You"
- "The [Topic] Quiz: What's Your Type?"
- "Find Out Your [Buzzword] Style In Just [Number] Questions"

## Example Headlines

- "What Kind Of Meeting Participant Are You?"
- "Take This Quiz To Find Out Your Email Sign-Off Personality"
- "Which Failed Startup Are You Based On Your Lunch Order?"
- "This Quiz Will Reveal Your Hidden Synergy Style"
- "What Your Coffee Order Says About Your Management Philosophy"

## Article Structure

- Brief intro promising self-knowledge
- Numbered questions with multiple choice
- Choices are all stereotypes
- Results section with 3-5 "types"
- Each type is either flattering or relatable
- Call to share results (for engagement)
- "What's your type?" prompt
