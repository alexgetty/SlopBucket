# Required Phrase: Choose violence

You must use this phrase at least once. Committing fully to confrontation or harsh judgment, usually about trivial things treated with stakes.

**Examples:**
- "When someone orders a cappuccino after 11 AM, I choose violence in my heart. You know why you're ordering wrong."
- "The LinkedIn influencer chose violence against the concept of 'work-life balance' in his 23-slide carousel."
- "She looked at his startup pitch deck and chose violence. Every word she spoke was justified."
