# Open Letter

A letter addressed to someone or something that will never read it. The performance of public address to an audience that isn't the addressee. Passive-aggression masquerading as earnestness.

## Headline Templates

- "An Open Letter To [Entity That Won't Read This]"
- "Dear [Abstract Concept]: [Grievance]"
- "A Letter To My [Younger/Older] Self About [Topic]"
- "To [Group]: [Demand They Won't See]"
- "Dear [Thing]: We Need To Talk"
- "An Open Letter To Everyone Who [Does Thing I Dislike]"
- "To The [Person] Who [Minor Grievance]: [Disproportionate Response]"
- "Dear [Concept]: It's Not You, It's Me (It's Actually You)"

## Example Headlines

- "An Open Letter To The Algorithm"
- "Dear Hustle Culture: We Need To Break Up"
- "A Letter To My Pre-Pandemic Self About Meetings"
- "To Everyone Who Replies-All: Please Stop"
- "Dear Morning Routine: You Lied To Me"

## Article Structure

- "Dear [X]," opening
- Establish relationship/grievance
- Acknowledge their perspective (then dismiss it)
- Escalate to broader life lessons
- Get increasingly personal/unhinged
- Sign off with passive-aggressive warmth
- Signature with unnecessary credentials
