# Ad Hominem

Attack the person, not the argument. Their character flaws invalidate their points.

**Examples:**
- "Of course they'd say that, they're a..."
- "Consider the source"
- "Easy for someone like you to say"
- "Your coffee order tells me everything I need to know"

**The messenger is not the message. But shooting them is easier.**
