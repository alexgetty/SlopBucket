# Format: Plot Twist Required

Must include a "plot twist" revelation that reframes everything. The LinkedIn M. Night Shyamalan.

**Structure:**
- Build toward one conclusion
- Drop the twist
- "But here's what I didn't tell you..."

**Examples:**
- "That company? It was mine."
- "The person who rejected me? Now works for me."
- "Plot twist: I was the toxic one."
