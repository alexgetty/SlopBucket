# Witnesses

People who saw things happen with their own eyes, which makes them either essential to truth or dangerously biased depending on whether their account supports the preferred narrative. Memory is famously unreliable except when it confirms what authorities already believe.

**Keywords:** eyewitness, testimony, accounts, observers, bystanders, contradicted, corroborated
