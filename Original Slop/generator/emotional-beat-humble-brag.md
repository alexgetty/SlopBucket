# Emotional Beat: Humble Brag

Disguise flexing as vulnerability. Complain about success. Make achievements sound like burdens.

**Examples:**
- "The hardest part of my third exit was..."
- "I'm so exhausted from all the speaking invitations"
- "It's lonely at the top of the Forbes 30 under 30"
